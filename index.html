About Me

Welcome! I'm Christian Villanueva.

I’m a Computer Engineering student who likes understanding how systems work by building them from the ground up. I’m most comfortable when I can see how individual components—hardware, operating systems, and networks—interact to form a larger system.

I spend a lot of my time experimenting in lab environments and home infrastructure projects. I build things, misconfigure them, break them, and then take them apart until I understand why they failed. That process is how I learn best. Documentation and repeatability matter to me, especially when I need to rebuild something after pushing it too far.
